About The Event

ArtConnect is a unique workshop designed to help art students connect with local artists and each other.

For our first workshop, Seattle based printmaker Jite Agbro will be sharing her experiences building a sustainable art practice. We'll also feature a selection of art provided to us by Seattle Central College art students.

Join us on December 1st at 1:30pm to kick off our event!

Our Speaker

Jite Agbro is a Seattle printmaker who's been making art for over twenty years. The focus of her work is non-verbal comacnication, the process of exchanging shared cultural, psychological, and imaginative cues between people.

From her website:

Her work explores the ritual of exchanging shared cultural cues through the use of status symbols (like clothing) and other forms of nonverbal comacnication. “I’m interested in how we as human beings project ourselves and our identities into the greater public space and how doing so changes the way we see ourselves and others.”

For more information about Jite Agbro: Visit Jiteagbroart.com
